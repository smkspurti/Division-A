## **Overview**

The Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023 (BNS) is the new Indian penal code, replacing the Indian Penal Code of 1860\. This dataset provides a structured, machine-readable version of the BNS, including chapters, sections, and descriptions.

## **Dataset Structure**

* File: (Replace with actual filename, e.g., bns\_sections.csv)  
* Columns:  
  * Chapter: Chapter number  
  * Chapter\_name: Name of the chapter  
  * Section: Section number  
  * Section\_name: Title of the section  
  * Description: Full text of the section

## **Data Source**

The dataset is based on the official gazetted version of the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, 2023, as enacted by the Indian Parliament. It is parsed and organized for research and computational use.

## **Use Cases**

* Criminal law research and analytics  
* NLP and AI-based legal applications  
* Legal education and curriculum development  
* Comparative studies with the Indian Penal Code
